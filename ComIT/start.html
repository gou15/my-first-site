You're ready to Play?

You're going to have to follow these steps to get yourself ready to play:

  1. Legally acquire a ROM.
  2. Header your ROM using this tool.

    A header is a line of code that gets written into the ROM files itself and allows your ROM to accept patches. It has no other effect on the ROM nor its playability.

  3. Clean your ROM using this tool.

    Checks the version of the ROM, checks that it's headered, and corrects any potential problems with the ROM

  4. Download this tool which will allow you to write patches to your ROM.

    When you write a patch to your headered and clean ROM file this tool will save your patched ROM as a new ROM file. Reminder - do not share ROM files and do not sell your ROM hacks

    Click on "Apply Patch," then choose the patch you want to apply, then chose the ROM you want to apply that patch to, THEN choose the directory to save the patched ROM file to.

  5. Get yourself an emulator.

    We linked you to SNES9x as it is the nearest representation to original SNES hardware available at the time of publishing. There are other emulators available but we recommend against them

    It's possible to play these games on a hacked SNES Classic, a Super NT, or on an original SNES using an SD2SNES cartridge... but we're cheap and an emulator is the free way to do it!

  6. Download the patch you want to apply

    Recommendations for beginner hacks at the bottom of this page. Click here to take yourself to a nice repository of many hacks

  7. Apply that patch to your clean ROM (see steps 3 and 4 again), open that ROM with your emulator, and get dying!

    Using the keyboard is a nightmare. You should invest in a controller if you're going to keep using an emulator. Look into an 8BitDo, or use an XboX controller


You're a Kaizo player now! Start with something easy so you're not extremely frustrated right away. We highly recommend:

  1. Panga's Kaizo KinderGarten - play this first. This will teach you so much more than this site has taught you. Panga loves Kaizo and he wants more people playing. This is taking lessons from the master himself.
  2. Baby Kaizo World - play this next. It's a step up in difficulty from Kaizo KinderGarten, and it's going to make fun of you when you fail a shell jump, but it's also a great beginners hack.
  3. Quickie World 1 and Quickie World 2 - another step up in difficulty, although still very forgiving. These levels give the player a good challenge, but it's over quick since the levels are short.

With those 4 hacks out of the way you're ready to go where you want. Jump right into some extremely difficult hacks with Grand Poo World 1, Super Panga World, or Super Dram World 2. If you want to just keep slowly upping the difficulty check out Akogare Mario World, Legends of Mario, or Korosu Mario World. If you're insane go ahead and try Invictus, Sheffy World 2, or Thumbshredder. Reputable hacks include: The Grand Poo World Series, Super Gracie World, Super Dram World Series, Claustrophobia, Super Ryu World Series, Salty Sagan World, Fly Me To The Stars, Super GlitchCat World, Lord Pickle World, Kaizo Mario World Series, and the Sheffy World Series.

You can find all sorts of hacks here, but this is not an exhaustive list of available hacks. Check out YouTube and Google to see what else might interest you.

Sincerely, good luck, I hope you enjoy it!
