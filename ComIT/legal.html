Who You Are

You've never heard of kaizo before, or you've only just started to discover it. Maybe you know a few of the words, or you've seen a YouTube video or two of extremely difficult Super Mario World levels. Maybe you're a rom hacker from another game altogether. Maybe you're looking to play your own Kaizo Mario, or you're an aspiring game designer looking to get some practice building your own levels. Or maybe you just Google'd "really really hard video games because I need a challenge." Either way, you're here because you want to learn more about Kaizo Mario.


Who We Are

We are passionate about Super Mario World Kaizo. We aim to be the destination for people who are looking to learn, watch, or play Kaizo Super Mario World games. If you're already a Kaizo veteran please visit our Resrouces page to take you to more advanced Kaizo information. We're here to teach some of the fundamentals, explain some of the words, answer some questions, and provide links to resources for you to learn more. You won't find advanced Kaizo tactics nor indepth level building instructions here, but we'll show you where to find them. We want to introduce people to the world of Kaizo, let them find what interests them, and pursue their interests further.


How To Get Around

Use the NavBar at the top of the page to navigate the site. Here's what you'll find:

HOME - you're here right now. Hello!

HISTORY - a look at where Kaizo started, how it's evolved, some key moments and people, and where it is today.

FAQ - frequently asked questions (is Kaizo Mario even legal? Short answer is "yes"). If you don't get an answer to your question here send us a message and we'll either add it or show you where to find it.

DEFINITIONS - the Kaizo community has come up with their own words for a lot of things. What's a regrab? What's a frame rule and why do I care? Find your answers here.

PLAYERS & PERSONALITIES - information about and links to influential streamers, YouTubers, and creators for you to learn from and be entertained by.

START PLAYING - think you've got the cajones to take these games on yourself? We'll get you started with the tools you need and recommendations on which games to start playing - no money required.

OTHER RESOURCES - we don't claim to be the definitive source of all things Kaizo. If you want to learn more this page will point you in the direction you need to go.
